OUTDOOR FESTIVAL OF DIGITAL ANIMATION AND ART DOWNTOWN DENVER SATURDAY SEPTEMBER 24TH
Denver Digerati will host the first ever outdoor festival of digital animation and art on September 24th at the Denver Performing Art Complex and the 14th Street LED screens at Champa and Arapahoe. Presented with support from our Galaxy Sponsors Denver Arts and Venues Next Stage NOW! / Denver Theatre District / CU Denver. Funded in part by the Bonfils-Stanton Foundation, cultivating arts and leaders for a vibrant community. Additional support from the Biennial of the Americas, Orange Barrel Media and WeedenLaw.
